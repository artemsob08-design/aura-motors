What is Starry Sky?

Starry Sky is a custom headliner lighting installation created to give the cabin a more premium and atmospheric feel. It combines hundreds of fibre optic light points with colour control and optional comet effects for a unique interior result.

This option is designed for drivers who want something more than a standard interior. The final result depends on the number of stars, the vehicle roof layout and the chosen configuration, but the goal stays the same — to create a clean, refined and visually impressive ceiling finish.

Main features

The installation can be customised depending on the vehicle and the desired look.

600+ stars

Installations start from 600 stars and can be scaled up depending on the desired density and final look.

Colour control

Multiple colour-changing modes are available to match the mood and interior style of the vehicle.

Remote & app control

The system can be controlled through a remote and phone app for convenient use and quick adjustments.

Optional comets

Shooting stars / comets can be added as an extra option for a more dramatic and premium visual effect.

Fitment & installation notes

A few important points clients should know before booking.

Vehicle fitment

Starry Sky installation can be done in most vehicles. In some sedans, removing the headliner properly may require additional work involving the windscreen, which affects the final price.

Installation time

Average installation time starts from 4 days and depends on the vehicle, roof structure and number of stars selected.

Pricing

Starry Sky installations start from €600. Final pricing depends on the number of stars, vehicle type and any additional work or options selected.

  • Optional comets / shooting stars — extra
  • Additional roof access or windscreen-related work, if required — extra
  • Final quote depends on the car and chosen configuration
