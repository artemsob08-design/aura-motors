What we inspect

Safety & road condition

We check important safety-related items that are commonly inspected during NCT.

  • Lights, indicators and brake lights
  • Tyre condition and visible damage
  • Brakes and basic braking condition
  • Suspension and steering checks
  • Leaks and visible underbody issues

Diagnostics & warning lights

If your car has warning lights or electrical issues, diagnostics can help identify the problem before the test.

  • Fault code scan
  • Engine warning light check
  • ABS / airbag related warnings
  • Electrical fault finding
  • Advice on the next repair step

Service condition

We can also check basic service items and help prepare the vehicle so it is cleaner, safer and more ready for inspection.

  • Oil and filter condition
  • Fluid level checks
  • Battery and charging check
  • Wipers, washers and visibility items
  • General advice before the test
