Paintless Dent Repair (PDR)

Paintless Dent Repair is a professional method of removing dents from a car body without repainting the panel. This technique keeps the original factory paint and restores the body shape quickly and safely.

No Repainting

The original factory paint remains untouched.

Fast Repair

Most dents can be repaired much faster than traditional body work.

Cost Effective

PDR is usually cheaper than conventional dent repair.

Clean Result

The panel returns to its original shape without fillers or paint.
