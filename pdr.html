What we check

Dent inspection

Not every dent is suitable for PDR. We check the size, location, paint condition and panel access before deciding what can be done.

  • Dent size and depth
  • Paint condition around the damage
  • Panel material and shape
  • Access behind the panel
  • Best repair approach

Paintless dent correction

When the dent is suitable, we work carefully to bring the panel back closer to its original shape without repainting.

  • Small dent correction
  • Car park dents
  • Minor body imperfections
  • Door dings
  • Clean panel finish

When PDR is a good option

PDR is often best for minor dents where the paint is not cracked and access to the panel is possible.

  • Minor dents without paint damage
  • Shallow dents on accessible panels
  • Factory paint still in good condition
  • Customers who want a cleaner repair option
  • Cases where repainting is not necessary
